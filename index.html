About the Artist

I create my art using many different mediums and have sold my miniature sculpts on eBay since 2013. This is my website that allows me to compile most of my work in one hub and provides links to areas where I showcase my products. I live in Wyoming and love learning and creating. I am open to discussion about commissioned work if you are interested. Currently I am in the market for a good literary science fiction author's agent and or an artist's agent or marketing partner.
